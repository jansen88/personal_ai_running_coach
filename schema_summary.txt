Table: runs

Columns:
- start_date (text): start date of run
- distance (float): total distance in kilometres
- duration (float): total duration in minutes
- pace (float): average pace per kilometre (min/km)
- elevation_gain (float): total elevation gain
- time_z1, time_z2, ..., time_z5 (float): time in minutes in each of the 5 heart rate zones
- pace_z1, pace_z2, ..., pace_z5 (float): average pace (min/km) in each of the 5 heart rate zones
